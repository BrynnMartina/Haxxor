Hacking for Social Good: Our Problem

In response to the "Hack for Social Good" challenge, our team is excited to present a mistake analysis software designed to revolutionize the learning process, particularly in the realm of math education. Recognizing the significant challenges faced by students in understanding and rectifying mistakes in their problem-solving approaches, we set out to create a product that goes beyond conventional tutoring methods. Our mistake analysis software utilizes advanced pattern recognition algorithms to dissect how individuals approach and solve math homework problems. By capturing the nuanced steps and methods employed by users, our software identifies recurring mistakes in their problem-solving journey.

The innovative aspect lies in its ability to not only pinpoint errors but also generate customized problem sets that specifically target the identified weaknesses. This ensures a tailored learning experience, focusing on the areas where individuals tend to stumble. Our product aims to empower learners by providing them with targeted and constructive feedback, fostering a deeper understanding of mathematical concepts and enhancing their problem-solving skills. Through this endeavor, we believe we can make a lasting impact on education, promoting inclusivity and offering a valuable tool for both students and educators alike.
